This Privacy Policy explains how the Opin browser extension (hereinafter referred to as "the Extension") handles user information. The Extension operates on the principle of not collecting or storing any personal data from users.

## 1. Personal Information Collected

The Extension does not collect personal information in any form. It also does not store or transmit identifiable information such as browsing data, cookies, or visit history.

## 2. Data Processing Method

The Extension works entirely within the user's browser. Processing related to detecting the availability of original images is performed locally. No information is transmitted to the developer or any external server.

## 3. Third-Party Sharing

The Extension does not provide or share personal information with any third parties.

## 4. External Servers and Communication

The Extension does not communicate with external servers. Detection of original images uses only resources available within the Pinterest webpage. Therefore, there is no possibility of transferring personal data or metadata externally.

## 5. Logging and Data Storage

No log data of any kind is stored.

## 6. Use of Permissions

The Extension accesses the DOM structure of the current Pinterest page in order to determine whether an original image exists. This permission is used solely for functional purposes and is unrelated to personal data processing.

## 7. Protection of Children's Information

The Extension does not collect personal information from children, nor does it intentionally handle information of users under the age of 14.

## 8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be modified when necessary. In the event of any changes, updates will be posted on the relevant listing page.
